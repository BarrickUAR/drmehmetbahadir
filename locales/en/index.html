Psoriasis

Psoriasis is a skin disease characterized by redness and scaly patches on the skin. Approximately 1 million people in Turkey have psoriasis. Although it appears to be contagious due to its symptoms, it is not a contagious disease.

Learn More...
02

Eczema

Eczema is a type of skin disease. Although it is not dangerous, it is a common condition. Studies show that 1 in 12 people have experienced eczema at some point in their lives.

Learn More...
03

Lichen Planus

Lichen Planus is a skin disorder that affects different parts of the body. It is believed to have a psychological trigger, but no definitive cause has been identified. It commonly appears on the inner skin, around the genitals in both men and women, as well as on the arms and legs.

Learn More...
04

Ichthyosis

Ichthyosis Vulgaris, also known as fish scale disease, is a skin disorder. It occurs when dead skin cells build up on the skin, creating a scaly, fish-like appearance.

About Us

Who is Dr. MEHMET Ilteber BAHADIR?

Born in 1971 in Antalya, he completed his primary and secondary education at Barbaros Primary School (Antalya) and Antalya Anatolian High School. From 1989 to 1996, he studied at Istanbul University Faculty of Medicine. He participated in Ministry of Health training programs on Mesotherapy and Ozone Therapy. He worked with the Leukemia Children’s Foundation and in 112 emergency services. Throughout his career, he has worked as a physician in various private hospitals.
