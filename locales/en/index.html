Our Services

01

Psoriasis

Psoriasis is a disease that causes redness and rash-like symptoms on the skin. It is a non-contagious disease, although it is often mistaken as contagious due to its visible symptoms. It affects around 1 million people in Turkey.

02

Eczema

Eczema is a skin condition, but it is not a dangerous disease. It is a common condition, affecting about 1 in 12 people. It is a widespread disorder without any major risk.

Learn More...
03

Lichen Planus

Lichen Planus is a skin disease. It is believed to have a psychological origin, though there is no definite cause. It commonly affects the skin, especially around the sexual organs and limbs.

Learn More...
04

Ichthyosis

Ichthyosis Vulgaris, commonly known as fish scale disease, is a skin condition where dead skin cells accumulate, forming a scaly appearance resembling fish scales.

About Us

Who is Dr. MEHMET İLTEBER BAHADIR?

He was born in Antalya in 1971. He completed his primary and secondary education at Barbaros Primary School (Antalya) and Antalya Anatolian High School. He studied at Istanbul University Faculty of Medicine between 1989 and 1996. He participated in Health Ministry trainings on Mesotherapy and Ozone Therapy. He worked with the Leukemia Children's Foundation and volunteered in 112 Emergency Services. He has worked as a physician in many private hospitals.
